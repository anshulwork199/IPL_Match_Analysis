# IPL Match Analysis
- Mumbai, Chennai, and Kolkata have the most wins (144, 138, 131 respectively) across all seasons suggesting a consistent strategy and squad performance.
- Gujarat Lions had the highest chance of winning a match if they won the toss, winning 66.67 % of matches after winning the toss. However, it is a young franchise having played fewer seasons than its peers.
- Pune had the lowest chance of winning a match if they won the toss, losing 85% of their matches after winning the toss. It suggests a failure to elect a choice based on their team’s strengths and a lack of awareness about weaknesses.
